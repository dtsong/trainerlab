## Visual Identity Direction

### Colors

| Color                             | Use              | Reasoning                                           |
| --------------------------------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Deep blue**                     | Primary          | Trust, intelligence, research                       |
| **Bright accent** (teal or green) | Highlights, CTAs | Energy, discovery, Pokemon-adjacent without copying |
| **Clean white/light gray**        | Backgrounds      | Readability, modern feel                            |
| **Dark gray/charcoal**            | Text             | Professional                                        |

Avoid: Pokemon official red/yellow (legal), overly dark "gamer" aesthetics (excludes parents)

### Typography

- **Headings:** Clean sans-serif, slightly bold (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans)
- **Body:** Highly readable (system fonts or similar)
- **Data:** Monospace accents for stats/numbers

### Visual Elements

- Clean data visualizations (not cluttered)
- Card images where relevant (proper attribution)
- Subtle lab/research motifs (grid patterns, subtle beakers/flasks in illustrations)
- **Potential mascot:** A Pokemon-professor-inspired character? Or keep it clean and professional.
